A Brief

While enhancing my skills of Artificial Intelligence I started to learn more about Human behavior, Mood Swings, various Psychological disorders. But all I was mostly restricted to blogs and posts but then I thought about extending my interest and I joined the 7 Cups Community, where I started by taking a bunch of courses and below I am summing up some important points that I learned.

  • I first took the Listener’s Course, in which they taught us about how to talk to a person having mental illness, what are the Correct Techniques to listen to them and how to motivate them towards the bright side of the situation.
  • Then I took the Counsellor Course, which I am still working on. The course is an extension of Listener’s course, teaching us about what can be the Best possible suggestions we can give to others.
  • I also took a bunch of Concentrated training courses, which talks about a particular life situation or a particular disorder, How to deal with it and what possible practices can help. The courses were about handling
    • ADHD
    • Anxiety
    • Bipolar Disorder
    • Breakups and Breakdowns
    • Depression
