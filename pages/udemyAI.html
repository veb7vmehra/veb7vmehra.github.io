A Brief

After gaining this conceptual knowledge I came to find out that there are still many complex AI/ML algorithms that I am not fluent with, and I need a bit more coding practice to achieve complete confidence in my skill. That’s why I enrolled into “Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Training Program” by Udemy. In the training course I again started from the basics of the algorithms, Supervised and Unsupervised Learning.

Then leaving behind my comfort zone of using python as coding language I started working on Javascript, and by the end of the course learned a bunch of new and old algorithms. So here is a list of things I have learned:

  • Learned Javascript, a new coding language and learned to use Tensorflow.js, a Deep learning library which is considered best for creating AI models of smaller size and that have faster result predicting time.
  • From the start I was working with clustering algorithms and even Neural Networks but while doing this training course I dug down the force working behind that, which is the technique of Pattern Recognition.
  • I worked on the Mathematical end of algorithms by having weekly classes on Applied Statistics during the training course.
  • I have been introduced to the concepts of Decision Trees, which lays the foundation of unsupervised deep learning.
  • Lastly I learned some of the most advanced Deep learning Algorithms, namely NEAT Algorithm and Q Learning Algorithm, I further created a practice project on Q Learning Algorithm trained to play the famous Asteroid Game.
